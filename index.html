Approach

Architecture as a Response

Each project begins with its context. We approach architecture as a response to place — shaped by landscape, orientation, and the conditions of everyday life.

Our work is grounded in materiality, proportion and clarity. Natural textures, light and spatial flow are used to create environments that feel calm, balanced and intuitive.

Through a precise and considered process, we develop each project from concept to construction, ensuring consistency at every scale. The aim is to create spaces that are not only functional, but deeply connected to the way they are experienced and lived.

Eva Sofoulaki

Architect Engineer NTUA  ·  Crete & Athens
